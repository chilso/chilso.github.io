Our rehearsals take place every Sunday at 9:30 am for two hours in downtown Chicago at 1440 North Lake Shore Drive. Free parking will be available for orchestra members.

Fall term time: 9/18/2016 - 12/11/2016

All you need to bring to the rehearsals is your instrument, a music stand, and your sheet music (which we will send to you in advance).
